QUESTION

What is a **closure** in JavaScript?

Click on the card to reveal the answer.
ANSWER

A closure is the combination of a function bundled together (enclosed) with references to its surrounding state (the lexical environment). This allows a function to access variables from an outer scope after the outer function has finished executing.
